7 ## How to install
8 To play the game, you just need to run the install.sh script.
9 Once the game is built and launched, it will prompt a question for which level you want to play.
10 Just enter ``1`` , ``2`` or ``3`` , or another number if you created a different level in the *Levels* folder

13 ## How To Play
14 You can move the playership using the ``up``, ``down``, ``left`` and ``right`` arrow keys
15 and you can use the ``spacebar`` to fire some bullets.
16 
17 ## Dumb ways to die
18 - You will lose a point when an enemy hits you.
19 - You will lose two points when you hit the ground or the super toxic sky.
20 - You can actually fly on the ground and through the clouds, but
21 the longer you stay there, the more points you'll lose.
22 - You will lose two points when you hit or fly through a Pipe (sporadic obstacle)

24 ## Easy way to win
25 - Each level has a certain amount of Enemy Waves. When you survive 'em all, you WIN!
